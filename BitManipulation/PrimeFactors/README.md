# Prime Factors

Given a number **n**, find its **unique prime factors** in increasing order.

---

## Example 1:

Input: n = 100

Output: [2, 5]

Explanation:  
Unique prime factors of 100 are 2 and 5.

---

## Example 2:

Input: n = 60

Output: [2, 3, 5]

Explanation:  
Prime factors of 60 are 2, 2, 3, 5. Unique prime factors are 2, 3, and 5.

---

## Constraints:

- 1 ≤ n ≤ 10⁶
